Energy & Mobility

The MoU with Catapult UK allows us to create test beds for innovators in the energy transition space. Behavioural Science

The Cycle To Work leaderboard uses gamification to make bicycle commutes count. Well designed leaderboards/dashboards serve as incentives to make behaviour change happen. Our association with BYCS, Netherlands has allowed us to take the leaderboard to the global stage.
